Stand With Ukraine

On Feb 24, 2022, Russia launched a full-scale war against Ukraine, causing immense suffering for the Ukrainian people. In response, many organizations and individuals have come forward to show their support and solidarity with Ukraine.

One way to help is through donations to various foundations and charitable organizations that are working to support Ukraine.

  • Leleka Foundation
  • The Come Back Alive Fund
  • Nova Ukraine
  • Razom
  • Charity foundation of Serhiy Prytula
  • Ukraine Armed Forces

You can also check #StandWithUkraine website for more information and ways to help.
